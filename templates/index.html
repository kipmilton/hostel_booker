Eastern Zone Hostels

Home to various male and female hostels, including Mfumbiro, Nyandarua, and New Menengai. Offering a range of room options for comfort and convenience.

Nyayo Zone Hostels

The most populous zone, located across the railway line. It consists of six hostels, including Nyayo One to Six and Nyayo Flats, accommodating both male and female students.

Western Zone Hostels

Located between the KU Conference Centre and the Students' Centre, Western Zone offers a variety of hostels such as Ngong, Ruwenzori, and Usambara for both genders.

Ruiru Campus Hostels

Designed for Self-Sponsored Students (SSP), Ruiru Campus provides hostels such as Chania, Athi, and Tana, catering to both male and female students.

Parklands Campus & Off-Campus Housing

Additional hostel accommodations available at Parklands Campus, with various options ranging from single to common rooms for different student needs.
